About Me

Hello, my name is Jisung Park (朴志晟). I'm from Seoul, South Korea, and I'm a member of the South Korean boy group NCT DREAM under SM Entertainment.

I was born on February 5, 2002, and I am the youngest member (maknae) of NCT DREAM. I debuted in 2016 and have been active as a dancer, rapper, and vocalist in the group.
